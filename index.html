Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries.

What kind of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in full-stack web development, mobile application development, and cloud-native solutions. My projects range from e-commerce platforms and data analytics dashboards to productivity tools and AI-powered applications.

What is your preferred technology stack?

While I'm adaptable, my preferred stack often includes React for frontend, Node.js or Python (Flask/Django) for backend, and databases like PostgreSQL or MongoDB. For mobile, I often use Flutter. I also have strong experience with cloud platforms like AWS and Azure.

How do you approach a new project?

My approach involves understanding the client's vision, user research, agile development with iterative feedback, and a strong focus on clean code, scalability, and security. I believe in transparent communication throughout the project lifecycle.

Can you help with existing projects or provide consulting?

Yes, I offer consulting services for architectural design, code reviews, performance optimization, and technology strategy. I can also contribute to existing codebases to enhance features or resolve issues.
